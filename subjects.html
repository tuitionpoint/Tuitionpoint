Comprehensive Subject Support

Every child’s academic journey is unique. At Tuition Point we offer expert tutoring across all core subjects and key stages, ensuring that whether your child is learning to read or preparing for GCSEs they receive consistent, high‑quality support. Our tutors are subject specialists who tailor lessons to meet your child’s needs, build confidence and ignite curiosity.

From English and mathematics to science and beyond, we provide structured programmes that align with the national curriculum and prepare students for assessments such as the 11+ entrance exam. With flexible one‑to‑one and small group options, we can design a learning plan that fits your family’s lifestyle.

Our Subject Areas

Explore our range of subjects below. Each programme can be tailored to Key Stage 1, 2, 3 or 4 and delivered one‑to‑one or in small groups.

English Tuition

From early phonics and handwriting to GCSE analysis of Shakespeare, our English tutoring nurtures confident readers and expressive writers. We cover reading comprehension, spelling, grammar, creative writing, literature, speaking & listening and exam essay planning.

Maths Tuition

Our maths tutors make numbers make sense. Whether your child is learning number bonds, fractions, algebra or calculus, we break down concepts into manageable steps and encourage logical thinking. We support preparation for SATs, times tables checks and GCSE exams.

Science Tuition

Science should inspire awe. Our lessons span biology, chemistry and physics across all key stages. We focus on practical experiments, scientific inquiry, critical thinking and preparation for combined and triple science GCSEs.

11+ Entrance Exam Prep

The Eleven‑Plus exam assesses verbal reasoning, non‑verbal reasoning, mathematics and English. We provide targeted practice in each area, teach strategies for multiple‑choice tests and build speed and accuracy to maximise scores.

GCSE & Exam Preparation

Our exam specialists guide students through the demands of GCSEs and other key assessments. We provide subject‑specific revision, teach exam techniques, administer mock exams and apply CRAVE revision strategies to cement learning.
