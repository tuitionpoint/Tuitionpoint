About Us

At Tuition Point, we believe every child deserves an education tailored to their unique abilities. Established by passionate and experienced teaching experts, our mission is simple — no child left behind. We provide exemplary education that is intricately personalised to each student’s learning style, fostering both challenge and motivation. Frustrated by outdated teaching methods, we set out to modernise tuition with innovative approaches that maximise attainment, confidence and long‑term success.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make personalised education accessible to students in London, Buckinghamshire and nationwide. By tailoring our approach to each learner, we build confidence, unlock potential and equip children with transferable study habits that last a lifetime.

Our Vision

We aim to pioneer the future of tutoring in the UK by turning lessons into mentorship and learning journeys into success stories. Our vision is a world where every student’s educational path is personalised, supported and designed for long‑term achievement.

Our Services

We cover a full range of tuition services from early years through GCSE preparation:

  • Academic Tuition – covering GCSEs, A‑Levels and 11+/13+ exams, our tutors provide targeted lessons that build knowledge, exam techniques and long‑term academic confidence.
  • 1‑1 Lessons – personalised tuition tailored to each child’s unique learning style, with every session focused on steady progress and lasting confidence.
  • Group Sessions – small, engaging groups where students collaborate, build confidence and learn through peer interaction.
  • Online & In‑Person Learning – families across London, Buckinghamshire and the UK can choose flexible online sessions or face‑to‑face tuition for hands‑on experience.
  • Personalised Mentorship – we go beyond tutoring; our mentors help students develop transferable study habits, resilience and self‑belief that last beyond the classroom.

Our Expert Tutors

Our team of expert tutors combines years of classroom teaching and private tuition experience across London, Buckinghamshire and the wider UK. Each tutor is carefully selected for their subject knowledge, teaching style and ability to adapt lessons to individual student needs. With a focus on building confidence, improving exam performance and encouraging a genuine love for learning, our tutors provide the personalised guidance every student deserves.
