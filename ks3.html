KS3 Tuition (Year 7–9)

Build confidence and academic strength in the early secondary years. At Tuition Point, we provide expert KS3 tuition in London for students aged 11–14 (Years 7–9). These years form the foundation of secondary education — a time when students adapt to new subjects, teaching styles and higher expectations. Our professional tutors make that transition smoother, helping every student strengthen subject knowledge, study habits and exam confidence while staying motivated and engaged.

Why Parents Choose Our KS3 Tutors

  • One‑to‑one & small‑group tuition for all major subjects
  • Qualified tutors specialising in English, Maths and Science
  • Tailored lessons that match the student’s school curriculum
  • Online and in‑person sessions across London
  • Regular feedback and progress reviews

KS3 Learning Priorities

  • Strengthening grammar, vocabulary and comprehension
  • Mastering core mathematical concepts and reasoning
  • Building scientific knowledge and analytical skills
  • Learning to study, revise and manage workloads effectively

Our Approach to KS3 Tutoring

  • Tailored Learning Plans: every child begins with a short assessment to identify current abilities and subject goals. Lessons are then tailored to align with their school curriculum and academic targets.
  • Developing Independent Learning: tutors help students build self‑discipline and study strategies so they learn not only what to study but how to study effectively.
  • Real‑World Application: lessons connect theory to real‑life examples, helping students see the purpose behind what they learn, from practical science experiments to analytical writing tasks.
  • Ongoing Progress Tracking: parents receive regular updates, feedback and term reviews, ensuring full visibility of their child’s progress and next steps.

KS3 Subjects & Skills We Cover

  • English & Literacy: analytical reading, essay writing, advanced grammar, comprehension, creative writing, literature analysis
  • Maths & Numeracy: algebra, geometry, fractions & ratios, data interpretation, equations, problem‑solving & reasoning
  • Science (Biology, Chemistry & Physics): forces & energy, chemical reactions, cell biology, scientific method, experiments & lab skills
  • Study Skills & Exam Preparation: time management, note‑taking, revision methods, test strategies, confidence & focus building

Help Your Child Thrive in Secondary School

Give your child the tools and confidence to succeed from the start of secondary school. Our KS3 tuition helps build solid foundations for GCSE success.
