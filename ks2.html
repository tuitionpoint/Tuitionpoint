KS2 Tuition (Year 3–6)

Strengthen skills and build confidence in the upper primary years. At Tuition Point, we provide expert KS2 tuition in London for students aged 7–11 (Years 3–6). These years are crucial for developing stronger reading comprehension, problem‑solving and exam readiness before moving on to secondary school. Our dedicated tutors deliver one‑to‑one and small group lessons, both online and in person, designed to boost academic progress and self‑belief — helping every child achieve their full potential.

What You Can Expect

  • Qualified and experienced KS2 tutors across London
  • Support in English, Maths and Science aligned with the National Curriculum
  • Preparation for SATs and 11+ exams
  • One‑to‑one or group learning options
  • Flexible scheduling around school and family life

KS2 Subjects & Skills We Cover

  • English & Literacy: reading comprehension, grammar & punctuation, essay writing, creative composition, vocabulary expansion
  • Maths & Numeracy: fractions, decimals, multiplication & division, geometry, data handling, problem‑solving & reasoning
  • Science: forces, electricity, light & sound, plants & habitats, scientific investigation, practical experiments
  • Exam Preparation (SATs & 11+): timed practice papers, revision planning, reasoning techniques, exam confidence & strategy

Our KS2 Tutoring Approach

  • Tailored Learning Plans: each child begins with a diagnostic assessment so we can design a plan that fits their strengths, goals and school curriculum.
  • Interactive Teaching: we use practical examples, visual aids and technology tools to make lessons more dynamic — helping children understand, not just memorise.
  • Exam Confidence & Study Skills: tutors teach effective study habits, test strategies and time management to prepare students for SATs and secondary school challenges.
  • Continuous Feedback: parents receive regular progress updates, ensuring full transparency and consistent improvement.

The Right Start for Lasting Success

Help your child reach their full potential and feel confident heading into secondary school. Our personalised KS2 tuition provides the support they need to thrive.
