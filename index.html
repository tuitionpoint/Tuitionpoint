About Tuition Point

124 City Road London, EC1V2NX

Our mission is simple: empower students to love learning and achieve their full potential. Whether your child needs support mastering the basics of reading and numeracy or is preparing for GCSEs and entrance exams, we’re here to guide them every step of the way.

How We Work

Free Consultation & Assessment

We begin with a free 30‑minute consultation and assessment to understand your child’s strengths, challenges and learning style. This helps us create a personalised plan tailored to their goals.

Personalised Tutor Matching

Based on the assessment we carefully match your child with a qualified tutor who specialises in their subject area and personality. Our tutors are experienced, DBS‑checked and passionate about teaching.

Ongoing Tuition & Mentorship

Your child benefits from structured sessions, regular progress reviews and ongoing mentorship. Our tutors adapt lessons as your child progresses, ensuring steady improvement and sustained confidence.

Key Stage Overview

Key Stage 1 (Years 1–2)

For early learners aged 5–7, we build strong foundations in reading, writing, numeracy and science. Lessons align with the national curriculum, supporting phonics, number bonds and curiosity about the world.

Key Stage 2 (Years 3–6)

Why Choose Tuition Point?

One‑to‑One Tuition

Individualised sessions provide targeted support and faster progress. Research shows that one‑to‑one tuition can significantly boost pupils’ outcomes, making it ideal for children who need focused attention.

Group Learning

Our small groups of 3–4 pupils foster collaboration and confidence. Students share ideas, motivate one another and develop essential social skills – a proven recipe for academic growth.

Flexible Online & In‑Person

Choose between online lessons, face‑to‑face sessions at your home or a convenient community space. We work around your family’s schedule and location, ensuring learning fits seamlessly into your routine.

Exam Preparation & Support

From SATs and Eleven‑Plus to GCSEs, our exam specialists teach test‑taking strategies, manage stress and guide your child through structured revision. We monitor progress and adapt lessons to maximise results.

Our Expert Tutors

Tuition Point was founded by passionate educators who believe every child deserves access to high‑quality support. Our co‑directors, including Morlai Happi, have years of experience teaching in UK schools and private tutoring. They oversee a hand‑selected team of subject specialists who are not only knowledgeable but also caring mentors. All tutors are fully vetted, DBS‑checked and trained to adapt their teaching style to each learner’s needs. Whether your child thrives on interactive discussions or step‑by‑step guidance, we’ll match them with a tutor who brings out the best in them.
