KS4 Tuition (Year 10–11)

Expert GCSE support to maximise potential. At Tuition Point, our KS4 tuition in London helps students aged 14–16 (Years 10 & 11) strengthen subject knowledge, master exam techniques and gain the confidence needed to succeed in their GCSEs. This is one of the most important stages of your child’s education — where academic foundations turn into measurable results. Our tutors provide structured, focused and encouraging tuition to help students reach their full potential in every subject.

Why Families Choose Our KS4 Tutoring

  • One‑to‑one & small‑group GCSE‑focused lessons
  • Expert tutors in English, Maths, Science and elective subjects
  • Aligned with GCSE & IGCSE specifications (AQA, Edexcel, OCR)
  • Tailored exam strategies and revision plans
  • Online or in‑person options across London

KS4 Learning Priorities

  • Developing revision routines and exam confidence
  • Mastering key topics for GCSE success
  • Understanding exam marking criteria
  • Practising past papers and timed assessments
  • Strengthening analytical, problem‑solving and writing skills

Our KS4 Tutoring Approach

  • Targeted Learning Plans: each student begins with a diagnostic assessment and consultation. We identify areas for improvement, align lessons with school syllabuses and create a roadmap toward exam success.
  • Exam‑Ready Teaching: every session includes practice questions, past paper analysis and mock tests — building familiarity with GCSE‑style questions and timing.
  • Revision & Strategy Coaching: tutors teach effective revision techniques, memory strategies and exam confidence skills — helping students manage stress and perform at their best.
  • Consistent Progress Tracking: parents receive updates and performance reports to monitor ongoing progress and outcomes.

KS4 Subjects & Skills We Cover

  • English Language & Literature: text analysis, essay writing, grammar & punctuation, poetry and Shakespeare studies, comprehension & critical interpretation
  • Maths: algebra, geometry, trigonometry, statistics, data handling, equations, problem‑solving, calculator & non‑calculator paper practice
  • Science (Biology, Chemistry & Physics): energy, forces, atomic structure, chemical reactions, genetics, ecosystems, practical experiments
  • Exam Preparation & Revision Skills: mock exams, past paper practice, time management, exam techniques, stress‑free revision & mindset coaching
  • Additional Subjects: tuition for optional GCSE subjects such as Geography, History, Business and Computer Science upon request
