Focusing on GCSE Success

Key Stage 4 is when students embark on the crucial two‑year journey towards their General Certificate of Secondary Education (GCSEs). Spanning Years 10 and 11, this stage requires focused study, effective revision and resilience. Pupils choose a combination of core and optional subjects, deepen their knowledge and develop the exam techniques needed to achieve their target grades.

At Tuition Point, our KS4 tutors specialise in helping students navigate this demanding period. We provide structured programmes in English, mathematics, science and elective subjects, combined with proven revision strategies and stress‑management techniques. Whether your child aims to climb several grade levels or secure top marks, we equip them with the tools for success.

What Is Key Stage 4?

Key Stage 4 covers Years 10–11 of secondary education. Students work towards national qualifications such as GCSEs, typically selecting between eight and ten subjects. The national curriculum specifies core subjects — English, mathematics and science — and foundation subjects including computing, physical education and citizenship. Pupils must also choose at least one subject from the arts, design & technology, humanities or modern languages.

GCSEs assess knowledge and skills through a combination of coursework and exams. Graded from 9 to 1, these qualifications influence Sixth Form placement, university opportunities and career pathways. Effective preparation, exam technique and stress management are therefore essential for KS4 students.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which GCSE subjects do you offer?

We offer tutoring across all GCSE subjects including English language & literature, mathematics (higher and foundation), combined and triple science, humanities, modern languages, business studies, economics, computing, creative subjects and more. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

How will tutoring improve my child’s grades?

We identify gaps in knowledge, teach exam‑specific techniques and build confidence through regular practice and feedback. Our personalised approach helps students understand what examiners expect, avoid common pitfalls and learn how to articulate their answers for maximum marks.

Do you provide mock exams?

Yes. Mock exams are a key part of our KS4 programme. We simulate exam conditions to help students manage time and stress, then provide detailed feedback to focus revision on areas that need improvement.

Can you help with coursework and controlled assessments?

We offer guidance on planning and structuring coursework, research skills and meeting assessment criteria. While students must produce their own work, our tutors provide feedback and support to ensure they’re on the right track.

When should my child start KS4 tutoring?

Early support is beneficial, but it’s never too late to start. Many students begin tuition at the start of Year 10 to build strong foundations, while others join in Year 11 for intensive revision. We’ll design a programme to fit your timeline.
