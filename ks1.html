KS1 Tuition (Year 1–2)

Give your child a confident start in primary school. We offer high‑quality KS1 tuition in London for children aged 5–7 (Years 1 and 2). These early years lay the foundation for a lifetime of learning, and our friendly, qualified tutors make sure every child starts strong. Through interactive, engaging lessons, we help students master core literacy and numeracy skills, build classroom confidence and develop curiosity for learning.

What Makes Our KS1 Tuition Different

  • One‑to‑one & small‑group primary tutoring (in person or online)
  • Aligned with the National Curriculum for Key Stage 1
  • Gentle, engaging approach that builds confidence
  • Regular progress tracking & parent updates
  • Flexible scheduling for busy families

KS1 Subjects & Skills We Cover

  • English & Literacy: phonics, reading comprehension, sentence writing, spelling, creative writing
  • Maths & Numeracy: counting, place value, number bonds, addition & subtraction, basic problem‑solving
  • Science: observation, comparison, experiments, asking “why” and “how” questions
  • Communication Skills: storytelling, vocabulary development, listening and speaking with confidence

Our Approach to KS1 Tutoring

  • Play‑Infused Learning: young children learn best through curiosity. Our tutors use storytelling, visuals and hands‑on games to make lessons interactive and enjoyable while reinforcing important concepts.
  • Personalised Learning Plans: every child learns differently. We begin with an academic assessment to identify their strengths, areas for improvement and learning style, then design a bespoke tuition plan around their needs.
  • Step‑by‑Step Progress: we introduce new topics gradually — moving from simple to complex concepts to ensure full understanding before progressing.
  • Confidence & Motivation Building: we focus on praise, encouragement and patience — helping children enjoy learning and take pride in their achievements.
  • Parent Collaboration: after every few sessions, we provide short progress updates and share practical ideas for reinforcing lessons at home.
